'Game of Life' Sim

May 2016

I was introduced to Conway's game of life in my first CS course. The simplicity of it's universe made it very accessible to me even though I had no programming experience and it is one of the first things that got me excited about computer science. Using JavaScript I created my own simple, but colorful, game of life simulator.
